How it works

Farmers can visit our nearest located collection centers or give us a missed call to sell their products directly.

Fresh food products will be taken directly from the farm. Farmers using the FarmO’Cart platform reportedly earn 15%-20% more than what they would if they sold the produce to mandis (markets).

Save time by avoiding visit to mandis. Get products at 5-10% less cost than that of mandi’s or wholesaler’s price

It will be brought in the collection and distribution centre of FarmO’Cart. Here it will be processed and packed by keeping hygienicses as the top most priority.

It will be sent to the neighbourhood medium-sized retailers and to the different people on their demand directly through distribution supply chain by reducing the middlemen.

Meet Our.....!!

The people who drive the core functions of FarmO'Cart.

Aastha Patel

Founder and CEO

Aastha Patel

Aastha founded FarmO'cart as an on-demand grocery delivery company in 2020 along with Ankit and Lina.She later realized the inefficiency of the current supply chain for perishable produce and pivoted the company to become an end to end B2B fresh produce platform. Aastha’s vision is to build India’s most efficient and largest supply chain company.

Ankit Patel

Co-Founder and COO

Ankit Patel

Ankit co-founded FarmO'cart in 2020 as a B2C hyper-local grocery delivery platform with Aastha and Lina. However, they soon realized that while there was a high demand for an online market for fruits and vegetables,and hence pivoted towards a B2B Supply Chain platform.

Lina Khedikar
